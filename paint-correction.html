Paint Correction

Restore your vehicle's finish to showroom condition with professional machine polishing.

1-Step Paint Enhancement

Our single-stage polish process is designed to bring your vehicle's finish back to life. By safely removing approximately 70-80% of paint defects, this polish will enhance your vehicle's finish and provide the depth and clarity to color you have been looking for.

Perfect for newer vehicles or those with minor imperfections like light swirls and water spots.

2-Step Paint Correction

A comprehensive 2-step paint correction process by machine polishing involves a cut/compound stage for defect and scratch removal, followed by a fine polish procedure to finesse the paintwork, adding depth and clarity to the vehicle's finish.

This process removes approximately 90% of defects in paint, including deeper scratches, heavy swirls, and oxidation. Best for heavily swirled or neglected paint.

$600 (all vehicles)
