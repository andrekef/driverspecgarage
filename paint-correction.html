1-Step Paint Enhancement

Our 1-Step "Paint Enhancement" service utilizes a single-stage polish to rejuvenate your vehicle's finish. This process effectively removes over 50% of paint defects, enhancing the depth and clarity of your vehicle's color. Ideal for those seeking a noticeable improvement in gloss and shine with minimal investment.

Price: $300 (car) - $500 (truck/SUV)

2-Step Paint Correction

The 2-Step Paint Correction involves a thorough machine polishing process. This includes a cutting/compounding stage to address defects and scratches, followed by a fine polishing stage to achieve a high level of depth and clarity. This service removes over 75% of paint imperfections, providing a show-quality finish.

Price: $600 (car) - $800 (truck/SUV)
