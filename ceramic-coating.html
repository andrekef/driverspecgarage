Ceramic Coating:

Protect your vehicle with a glossy, hydrophobic finish. Our Ceramic Coating service includes a thorough decontamination wash and a 1-Step Paint Correction to remove minor imperfections and swirls.

We apply Gtechniq Crystal Serum Light (CSL) as a base layer, which provides strong, long-lasting protection against UV rays, chemical etching, and scratches. On top, we apply two layers of EXO v5, which adds extra hydrophobic properties, water-beading, and gloss for easier maintenance.

With proper care and maintenance washes, this coating can last 3–5 years, keeping your paint glossy and protected.

Cure Instructions: 12 hours to set, avoid rain or washing for 4–7 days.

$600 (car) - $700 (truck/SUV)
