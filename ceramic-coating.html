Ceramic Coating:

Looking for long-lasting protection and a high-gloss finish for your vehicle? Our Ceramic Coating service begins with a thorough decontamination wash to remove embedded contaminants such as industrial fallout, brake dust, tar, and tree sap. We then perform a 2-Step Machine Paint Correction (Cut & Polish) to eliminate imperfections like swirls, scratches, and etchings, ensuring your vehicle’s finish is impeccable.

What makes our coating stand out? We use Gtechniq Crystal Serum Light (CSL) as a base layer, which offers unrivaled protection and durability. CSL is a professional-grade ceramic coating that creates a strong, chemically resistant layer over your vehicle's paint. This coating provides exceptional protection against environmental contaminants, UV rays, and minor scratches. On top of CSL, we apply two layers of Gtechniq EXO v5. EXO v5 enhances the finish with an additional layer of hydrophobic protection, adding an extra gloss and slickness while further defending against water spots, dirt, and grime.

Wondering how our ceramic coating benefits you? Our coating not only provides a hydrophobic layer that repels water, keeping your vehicle cleaner for longer, but it also enhances the paint’s depth and clarity. This results in a glossy, smooth finish that makes your car look new for years. It’s designed to last, reducing the need for frequent waxing and maintaining a high level of protection against the elements.
