Interior Steaming:

Looking to refresh and sanitize your vehicle’s interior? Our Interior Steaming service uses a McCulloch steamer to deep-clean and disinfect your car’s interior. This eco-friendly method targets vents, upholstery, and other surfaces, effectively removing dirt, light stains, and odors without relying on harsh chemicals.

How do we make your interior shine? We start by vacuuming loose debris to prepare the surfaces. Then, using the McCulloch steamer, we apply high-temperature steam to clean and disinfect areas like air vents, seats, and carpets. The steam lifts light stains and sanitizes surfaces, leaving your interior fresh and revitalized. We finish with a wipe-down to ensure a clean, polished look.

Why choose our steaming service? This service is perfect for maintaining a clean and healthy cabin environment. The steam cleaning process eliminates bacteria and allergens in vents and upholstery, improving air quality and comfort. It’s a quick, effective way to restore your vehicle’s interior, keeping it looking and smelling great.

$50 (vent cleanup and mildew removal from vents) - $200 (entire interior decontamination, deep vacuum of carpets, seat belts, and seats, no extraction)
